Nanorod Membrane Interaction
============================

This demo captures anisotropic nanorods interacting with a soft membrane, highlighting adhesion events and bending modes.
Frames zoom in on the interface to make subtle rotations and slip events visible for presenters.
The video is useful for illustrating how aspect ratio and surface chemistry influence membrane deformation.
It also serves as a placeholder for future quantitative overlays such as curvature maps.
