Bacteria 3D Motion
==================

This demo shows a swarm of motile bacteria tracked in three dimensions to illustrate helical swimming paths and tumbling behavior.
Depth cues and overlaid trajectories make it easy to pause on individual cells or highlight collective drift.
The clip is intended for talks where spatial context matters and for sanity-checking reconstruction quality.
Use it as a template for other volumetric tracking datasets.
