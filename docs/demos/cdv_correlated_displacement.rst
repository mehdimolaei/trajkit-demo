CDV Correlated Displacement
==========================

This demo presents correlated displacement vectors (CDV) extracted from a collective dynamics experiment.
Arrow overlays emphasize spatial coherence, while color encodes displacement magnitude to support quick interpretation.
The clip is intended for microrheology and active matter talks where correlations over time and space are discussed.
Use the page as a template for adding quantitative legends or annotations in future revisions.
