Bacterial Motion
================

Pseudomonas bacterium swimming from the bulk toward an oil-water interface, becoming trapped, and swimming in circles in the trapped state.
Trajectory are obtained by 3D reconstruction of digital holograms, making depth-resolved motion easy to visualize.
