Holographic Bacterial Tracking
==============================

Short time-lapse of bacterial holograms generated by denoised digital inline holographic microscopy, showing reconstructed trajectories in 3D.
For methodological context, see the inline holography approach described in `Optics Express 22(26):32119 <https://opg.optica.org/oe/fulltext.cfm?uri=oe-22-26-32119>`_.
